We hope our guests find true bengali hospitality in our resturant and happiness in our food.

Bistro resturant is a fine dining establishment. The resturant is the perfect hub for this Bengali theme. The resturant serves a seasonal menu, complete with ingredients from local farms and traditional dishes with a twist.

Come experience Bistro, where flavorful dishes, exemplary service, and bengali hospitality are in abundance.
